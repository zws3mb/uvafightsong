Fight Song Competition

Call for Works - Open until JUNE 1, 2015


 In 2015 the Vice Provost for the Arts and the McIntire Department of Music will be sponsoring a UVa Fight Song Competition. We are calling for UVa students to write new Fight Songs that capture the spirit of life here on grounds. Finalist songs selected from among the entries will be performed by the UVa Glee Club at a special event in October 2015. The songs will be judged by a panel of UVa faculty and student leadership at the event and prizes ($500 for 1st place, $250 for 2nd place, $100 for 3rd place) will be awarded to the winners.

Eligibility

The competition is open to song-writing teams of made up of 3 or fewer currently enrolled graduate or undergraduate students (all team members must be enrolled in both Spring and Fall 2015 semesters).

Prizes

        First place : $500, performance of your song by the Virginia Glee Club, and UVa sponsored recognition of your song.

         Second place: $150, and performance of your song by the Virginia Glee Club.

        Third place: $100, and performance of your song by the Virginia Glee Club.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • Lyrics must be original.
  • Music may be original or adapted from a melody in the public domain (please see here for more information about melodies in the public domain)
  • Each submission must include lyrics and an .mp3 recording.
  • The recording (.mp3) does not have to be performed by the authors.
  • Entries are encouraged to be approximately 2 minutes long (longer or shorter entries are discouraged but allowed).
  • Please submit online here. (← url. easy conference submission site?)
  • Teams may submit up to three works for consideration.
  • Deadline for submission is June 1, 2015.

Judging

  • The initial round of submissions will be reviewed by UVa alumni.
  • Up to X number of finalists will be selected.
  • Judging will be based upon lyrical content, school spirit, tradition, creativity, and singability
  • Only one prize awarded per team.
  • Finalists will be announced by August 1, 2015.

Finalists

  • Finalists will be asked to submit a musical score to be performed by the Virginia Glee Club.
  • Help with producing a score for those less familiar with music notation will be available.
  • Deadline for Finalist scores is August 25, 2015.

Questions may be directed to fightsong@virginia.edu.
